Our History

Founded in 1976

The most famous restaurant in Paris

Nestled in the heart of Paris, "Fiorente" is more than just a restaurant; it's a testament to the enduring spirit of Italian culinary tradition in the heart of the French capital. Established in the early 20th century by the visionary chef Giovanni Rossi, "Fiorente" emerged as a beacon of Italian gastronomy amidst the vibrant tapestry of Parisian cuisine.
Chef Giovanni Rossi, a native of Florence, brought with him a passion for the flavors and aromas of his homeland when he arrived in Paris at the turn of the century. Determined to share the authentic taste of Italy with the discerning palates of Paris, he opened "Fiorente" in a quaint corner of the city, overlooking the bustling boulevards and picturesque avenues.
